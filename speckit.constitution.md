# Saqr Project Constitution 🦅📜

## Vision
Saqr is a professional-grade Quantitative Investment Platform. Its primary goal is the autonomous management of capital through statistically proven patterns and rigorous AI-driven scientific debate.

## Immutable Rules (The Constitution)

1. **Quant-First Evidence:** No trading strategy shall be generated or deployed without a mandatory backtest against at least 10 years (or available maximum) of historical OHLCV data.
2. **Adversarial Debate:** Every strategy must pass through a minimum of 7 rounds of scientific debate, including a mandatory "Adversarial Attack" phase (The Guardian) to stress-test hypotheses.
3. **Portfolio Awareness:** No AI Worker (Hunter or Standard) shall operate in isolation. All workers must be aware of their capital exposure relative to the total portfolio to prevent liquidation.
4. **Technology Gating:**
   - **Reasoning:** Use Gemini 2.0 Flash for all council experts to maintain sub-60s session latency and minimal cost.
   - **Execution:** All trading operations must support both Paper Trading (Mock) and Live Trading (Real) with a strict manual switch.
5. **Halal Vision Compliance:** All scanned symbols must pass the `halal_vision` filter before any entry is considered.
6. **No Placeholder Logic:** Placeholder code in critical paths (entry/exit) is strictly forbidden. All logic must be statistically grounded.

## Success Metrics
- Average session completion time < 3 minutes.
- Backtest success rate stability > 55% across 3-year splits.
- Zero "Black Swan" liquidations in stress-test simulations.
